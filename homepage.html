About us

Golden springs is a private school that offers quality education for students from kindergarten to high school. We have a team of qualified and experienced teachers, a modern and well-equipped campus, and a variety of co-curricular activities. Our mission is to nurture the talents and potentials of our students, and prepare them for the future.

Our services

We offer a range of services to meet the needs and interests of our students. Some of our services include:

Curriculum

We follow the national curriculum of Kenya, and also incorporate international standards and best practices. We offer a balanced and holistic education that covers academic, social, and moral aspects.

Technology

We use technology to enhance the learning experience of our students. We have a computer lab, a library, and a smart board in every classroom. We also provide online learning platforms and resources for our students.

Sports

We encourage our students to participate in sports and physical activities. We have a spacious playground, a gym, and a swimming pool. We also offer various sports clubs and teams, such as football, basketball, volleyball, and chess.
